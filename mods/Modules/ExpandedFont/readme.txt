                                     ABOUT

ExpandFont ports the added font character functionality from the European and
Japanese versions of CTR to the American version, including in-line colors and
quotation marks.

                                CHARACTER USAGE

* In-line coloring: Tilde followed by a two-digit number, corresponding to the
  ID of the color you want to use.
  Example: ~04CHARACTER: ~05CRASH BANDICOOT -- "CHARACTER:" will be in white
  (ID 4) and "CRASH BANDICOOT" will be in blue (ID 5).

* Quotation marks: This one actually does use quotation marks.
  Example: you don't need it lmao
